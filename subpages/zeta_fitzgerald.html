Why Zeta Analysis?

Craig’s Zeta Test: It identifies words that are distinctively preferred or avoided in two compared corpora. In this case, the graphs highlight lexical preferences and avoidances in Scott's text relative to Zelda's texts.

Why Change Occurrence Threshold?

  • A threshold controls the frequency of words considered in the analysis.
  • At a lower threshold (2), more words are included in the comparison, which can capture rarer stylistic markers but may include noise.
  • Higher thresholds (4 and 10) focus on words that occur more frequently, potentially revealing more consistent stylistic patterns.
